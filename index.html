About Us

Partyrzzi Photo Booth is a unique one of a kind open-air photo booth that illuminates. The booth’s sophisticated display of color changing lights can be set to match your event’s theme.

Book us for your: Weddings, Anniversaries, Birthday Parties, Debutant Balls, Quinceaneras, Sweet 16s, Showers, Reunions, School Activities, Dances, Graduations, Fundraisers, Holiday Parties, Corporate Events, Conventions and many more!

Unlike the traditional closed photo booths with Partyrzzi it’s all about walking down the red carpet and being seen, so let us give you and your guests the VIP treatment for your special event.

Our booth can be placed indoors or outdoors it is equipped with an 18-megapixel DSLR camera and a top of the line dye-sublimation photo printer that produces your unlimited prints within 20 seconds and yes they will be dry to the touch creating the perfect keepsake for your special occasion.
